a self-initiative project by designer Mertygraal, also known as Makar Hrydkovets, about how quickly the price of crypto currency changes. The project itself lists its virtual non-existent crypto currency as starting at a price of 10 dollars

It is important that the price you see in front of you does not exist, but only imitates it.

The goal of the project is to visualize how quickly and unpredictably the price of a cryptocurrency can rise or fall to incredible levels.

The price started at $10, the project itself includes two servers that work on calculating and visualizing the price. The first server is responsible for generating the price and its increase or decrease. And the second server is created to display the price on the site so that everyone can go in and see the price now.

The project uses only files with the following extensions: js. ​​json. css. html.
